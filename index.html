What is lChaty?

lChaty is a family-first AI assistant designed to grow with your household. It helps with everyday tasks — from answering quick questions and making grocery lists to reading bedtime stories, helping with homework, and remembering family preferences. Our goal is to build a friendly, trustworthy assistant that everyone in the family can use.

Key goals

  • Safe and approachable: language, tone, and features designed for family use.
  • Helpful across ages: simple answers for kids, deeper assistance for adults.
  • Privacy-first: local-first options and careful data practices so families control their data.
  • Extendable: plugins and integrations for calendars, shopping lists, and smart home features.
